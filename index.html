The communication gap

Why Rehearsal Fails Without Feedback

Many students and young professionals practise alone, but rarely receive realistic, immediate, or structured feedback — leaving anxiety and uncertainty hidden until the real moment arrives.

Presentation anxiety

Users often know their material but struggle when pressure, nerves, and audience expectations appear.

Fear of judgement

Practising in front of others can feel exposing, especially before assessed or career-defining moments.

Unclear structure

Without feedback, speakers may miss gaps in logic, flow, timing, and message clarity.

Limited coaching access

Tutors, mentors, and career advisers are valuable, but they are not always available on demand.
